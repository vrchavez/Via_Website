30 Day Guarantee

Here at via phone repair, your satisfaction is our highest concern. That is why every repair comes with a 30 day guarantee. The guarantee covers any defect that a replaced screen or part may have. This does not include damage brought upon by client on screen or phone parts after repair. Does not include broken buttons or software malfunction that was not brought upon by one of our technicians. Every repair consists of a fully documented check of clients phone. This check include pictures and video of working and non-working parts of phone. If replaced part does not function properly, we here at via will replace the part, free of charge.
